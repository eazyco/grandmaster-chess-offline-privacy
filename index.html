Privacy Policy

GrandMaster Chess: Offline
Developed and published by Eazyco
Last updated: February 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how the mobile application GrandMaster Chess: Offline (“the App”) handles user information and data. The App is developed and published by Eazyco.

1. Purpose of the Application

GrandMaster Chess: Offline is designed to function without requiring internet access. All gameplay logic, game states, and AI interactions operate locally on the user’s device. The App does not require account creation, sign-in, or any form of user identification.

2. Data Collection and Usage

The App does not collect, store, process, or transmit any personal data, sensitive information, or device identifiers.

Specifically, the App does not collect:

  • Names, email addresses, or contact information
  • Location data or IP addresses
  • Device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • Usage analytics or behavioral data
  • Crash reports or diagnostic data

3. Internet and Network Access

The App does not require an active internet connection for normal operation. All features are available offline.

4. Advertisements

The current version of the App does not display advertisements and does not integrate any advertising SDKs or third-party ad services.

If advertisements are introduced in future versions, this Privacy Policy will be updated accordingly to reflect changes in data handling practices.

5. Third-Party Services

The App does not use third-party services such as analytics platforms, tracking tools, social media SDKs, or external APIs that collect or process user data.

6. Data Storage

Any data generated during gameplay, such as game progress or settings, is stored locally on the user’s device. This data is not backed up to external servers.

7. Children’s Privacy

The App is intended for users aged 13 years and above. The developer does not knowingly collect personal information from children. Since no data collection occurs, no personal information of any kind is processed.

8. User Rights

As the App does not collect or process personal data, users are not required to request access, modification, or deletion of data. All data remains local to the device and can be removed by uninstalling the App.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time to reflect changes in the App’s features, legal requirements, or business practices. Any updates will be published on this page, and the “Last updated” date will be revised accordingly.
